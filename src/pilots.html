How it works

From initial consultation to thriving research community in 4 weeks

    Initial Consultations
    Connect with our technical team to discuss your institutional needs, research community size, and deployment timeline. We'll assess technical requirements and answer questions about federated infrastructure.
  • Step 2

    We evaluate your existing IT infrastructure, security requirements, and integration needs. We provide a detailed technical specification and implementation plan tailored to your institution.
