Built for Science, Owned by Scientists

Scientific discourse shouldn't be held hostage by corporate platforms.

Open Science Network is federated infrastructure that researchers and institutions control.

Professional Identity

Sign in with ORCID. Your publications, affiliations, and research interests automatically populate from trusted sources like OpenAlex.

Connected Knowledge

Share a paper and watch it come alive: metadata, co-authors, datasets, and related work appear instantly. Colleagues can discuss directly in context or add them to channels.

Data Sovereignty

Your university hosts its own instance. Your data stays yours. Connect globally while maintaining local governance and values.

Archive with Zenodo

Conversations get DOIs and become citable FAIR objects. Valuable exchanges become permanent parts of the research record instead of disappearing in email or social threads.

Modular

Extensible by design. Add new repository connectors, peer review workflows, and research tools as your community grows. Built on Bonfire's modular framework for unlimited customization without vendor dependency.

Open Social Web

Connect universities, research organizations, and scientific communities across the open social web. Researchers at university servers collaborate seamlessly with open science advocates on Mastodon, science communicators on BlueSky, and specialized research networks, creating a truly global scientific discourse.
